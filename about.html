Railside Pizza located in Malta, Montana (population, 1,860) has been in business since 1986 selling pizza to the community. Railside has a lovely dining location on 1st Street (US Highway 2) in downtown Malta. During the COVID pandemic, Railside closed the doors to its dining room and begin taking phone orders for their pizzas. Customers can either pick up their pizza curbside or have it delivered.
